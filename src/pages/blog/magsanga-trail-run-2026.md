<p class="lead">
    A race that tested speed, strength, and endurance under unforgiving heat.
</p>

***

The course featured a mix of rugged trails and exposed road sections, pushing runners to manage both terrain and temperature. It was the kind of race that demands patience early and toughness late.

### Race Results
- Lilik Velasco – Champion, 12KM
- Nick Aguilos – 4th Place, 12KM
- Erika Viojan – Champion, 21KM
- Gil Peñero – 4th Place, 21KM

***

From the opening kilometers, our runners settled into strong rhythms. The climbs were relentless, and the road segments amplified the heat, but the team stayed composed and competitive throughout.

Two championship finishes and two strong 4th place efforts reflect the work that’s been happening long before race day — consistent training, focused preparation, and the mindset to compete.

### A Strong Start

Magsanga marks the beginning of our 2026 campaign. With bigger races ahead, this was an important first test — and a reminder that progress comes from showing up and putting in the work.

We’re proud of the performance. We’re proud of the effort. And we’re just getting started.
