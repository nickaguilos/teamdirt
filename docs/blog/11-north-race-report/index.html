11 Degrees North Race Report

Reported by Juan Nowan, Writer for Team Dirt on March 20, 2026

11° North Orienteering Championships 2025: Official Results

On March 20, under the heat of a Tacloban morning, athletes gathered for the 11° North Orienteering Championships—a race built to challenge navigation, decision-making, and composure under pressure.

From first-time racers to seasoned teams, the field brought a mix of speed, strategy, and a lot of wrong turns (the fun kind).

Here are the official results:

Elite Category

Taking the top spot in the Elite division, Bagtik showed strong navigation and consistent pacing throughout the course to secure the championship.

Final Rankings:

  • Bagtik – Champion
  • Apurado – 2nd Place
  • A+Plot Adventure Racing – 3rd Place
  • Old S Cool Dos – 4th Place
  • Sierra 2 – 5th Place
  • Strangers – 6th Place

Novice Category

In the Novice division, Run43 led the pack, proving that even newer teams can move fast and navigate smart.

Final Rankings:

  • Run43 – Champion
  • TH1 – 2nd Place
  • Old S Cool Uno / Navenshon – 3rd Place
  • Nitecore Silangan – 4th Place
  • Sierra 1 – 5th Place
  • Sinirangan Trail Quest – 6th Place
  • TH2 – 7th Place

More Than Just Results

11° North was never just about finishing first. This race is part of a bigger goal of ours: to add spice to the already growing navigation-based racing community, and to introduce more people to the challenge (and chaos) of orienteering.

What’s Next

We saw new faces, returning racers, and teams already asking for more.

So this isn’t the last.
