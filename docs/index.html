Team Dirt: Endurance Team From Eastern Visayas

Team Dirt is an adventure racing and outdoor endurance team from Eastern Visayas.

We race, explore, and build events that bring people outdoors — from mountain trails to coastal towns — carrying the same spirit of teamwork, movement, and discovery wherever we go.

We train together, compete across the country and beyond, and create opportunities for others to experience the same wild joy of being out there.
