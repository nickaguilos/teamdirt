Who we are

Team Dirt is a Philippine-based adventure racing team and outdoor community. We compete in elite-level races locally and internationally, while also organizing grassroots events and weekly community runs that grow the sport from the ground up.

at a glance
  • Adventure Racing World Series (ARWS) Asia Champions
  • Multiple awardees of local / regional races in Adventure Racing, MTB, Rock Climbing, Road and Trail Running
  • Race organizers and community builders
